HOW TO PLAY ?

> The game will display a blank field with five dashes representing the five letters of the unknown word.

> You have six attempts to guess the unknown word. Click or tap on the letters to guess the word.

> If a letter is correct and in the correct position, it will be displayed in green. If a letter is correct but in the wrong position, it will be displayed in yellow.

> If a letter is not in the word, it will be displayed in gray.

> Use the feedback from each guess to narrow down the possibilities of the word.

> Keep guessing until you have correctly guessed the word, or until you have used up all six attempts.
